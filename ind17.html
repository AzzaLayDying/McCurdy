Individual Note 2

Military Service and Prisoner of War

James (Jim) White served in the military. He was captured at Ruweisat Ridge, El Alamein in Egypt on 14 July 1942 when he was 40 years old.

 

29th and then 24th Infantry Battalion. Serial # 23005.

Individual Note 3

Death

James (Jim) White died on 17 August 1942 when he was 40 years old. He was 'Killed in Action', on Board the Italian ship the 'Nino Bixio' carrying Prisoners of War (POW).
